Strategy

See how a production company works. A quick recipe for your perfect video.

  • Step 1 : Pre Production

    Brain Storming / Story boarding

    The brief is a document that contains Master plan of the entire project. We come up with some creative ideas to suit your budget. We will present you with a script / storyboard / mood board / animatic. We will walk you through the idea, from start to it's end frame! After you sign off the brief document and location we will proceed ahead.

  • Step 2: Production

    Shoot

    This is the day (or days) of filming. Expect to see a crew full of energy, geared with suitable (legal) weapons. We will execute our master plan, and no show stoppers (Pray no rain ).

  • Step 3: Post Production

    EDITING,MOTION DESIGN, SOUND DESIGN

    We cut together the video and show you the first ‘rough cut’. The rough cut is passed to our motion design and graphics team, Where captions and special effects are added. Professional voice over is added in Dubbing. Music is added along with proper licenses, to avoid any hassles of distribution. We will keep you updated throughout the process.

  • Step 4: Delivery

    Video release / Handover

    The day you have been waiting for. We will share the output with you on the day of Delivery as per the agreement. With a promise to see you again
